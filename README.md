SHORT BIOGRAPHY

Onwumechiriuba Augustine Obinna, professionally known as O. A. Obinna Esq., is a Nigerian-trained legal practitioner with a solid academic and professional foundation in law. He obtained his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) degree from the University of Lagos (UNILAG), one of Nigeria's most prestigious institutions, before proceeding to the Nigerian Law School, Lagos Campus, where he was called to the Nigerian Bar in 2025.

Obinna completed his mandatory national service with the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) in Kogi State, where he served at the Kogi State Ministry of Justice before being transferred to the Office of the Public Defender & Citizens' Rights Commission (PDCRC).

Through these postings, he gained invaluable experience in public legal administration, civil rights advocacy, and access to justice, reinforcing his commitment to serving the most vulnerable members of society with diligence and professionalism.

A versatile legal practitioner, Obinna's areas of practice span Real Estate & Property Law, Corporate and Commercial Law, Criminal Law, and Family & Civil Law, equipping him to handle a broad spectrum of legal needs across individual, corporate, and public interests.
